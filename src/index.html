Sidewalk Priorities: Montgomery County, PA

This map was designed to allow transportation planners to take a deep dive into the pedestrian accessibility of essential services in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, building on top of the regional sidewalk gap analysis.

These essential services analyzed here were identified by combining datasets from two sources:

  • Points of interest from DVRPC's "Equity Through Access" study
  • Schools, libraries, municipal buildings, and historic sites from Montgomery County's open data portal

This map is divided into two parts:

  • Explore by Destination to find sidewalk gaps that would benefit specific places, like an elementary school or a grocery store
  • Explore by Muncipality to see all of the gaps that exist within a township or borough, and to identify how many places might benefit from filling a specific gap
