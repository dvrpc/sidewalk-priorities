Sidewalk Priorities: Montgomery County, PA

This map was designed to allow transportation planners to take a deep dive into the pedestrian accessibility of essential services in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania.
These services were identified by combining two datasets:

  • Points of interest from DVRPC's "Equity Through Access" study
  • Schools, libraries, municipal buildings, and historic sites from Montgomery County's open data portal

This map is divided up into two parts:

  • Explore by Destination to find sidewalk gaps that would benefit specific places
  • Explore by Muncipality to see all of the gaps that exist within a township or borough
